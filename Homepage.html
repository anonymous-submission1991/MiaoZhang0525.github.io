About Me:

Greetings! Dr. Miao Zhang is an Assistant Professor at Computer Science Department, Aalborg University (AAU), Denmark. Before that, Miao was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Machine Learning Group, FIT, Monash University, working with Prof. Gholamreza (Reza) Haffari, and Prof. Xiaojun Chang. He received PhD from FEIT (Thesis: Automated Deep Learning: A Study on Neural Architecture Search), University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Australia, working with Prof. Steven Su and Dr. Shirui Pan.

Email: miaoz@cs.aau.dk; miaozhang1991@gmail.com;

Research Interests

Recently, he focuses on the following topics, include:

  • Automated Machine Learning (AutoML), Neural Architecture Search, Hyperparameter Optimization

  • Bayesian Optimization, Bayesian Deep Learning

  • Meta Learning, Explainable AI

  • Graph Neural Network

  • Network Pruning, Knowledge Distillation, Network Quantization
